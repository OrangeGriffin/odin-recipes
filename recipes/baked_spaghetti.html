Baked Spaghetti

Description

This tasty dish combines two of your favorites. Spaghetti. And baked things.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Sacue or choosing
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Cheeses of your choosing

Directions

  1. Prepare spaghetti
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Combine all ingredients
  4. Bake at 0 Kelvin for 5 hours
